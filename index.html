Goals:



Enhance the reading experience for Readly.

Attract a younger target group (millenials).







Focus question:



How can Readly enhance the reading experience for millenials?





Approach:

5 Weeks.



Mentored by industry leaders from the agency Designit.



The client co-created the workshop with us as they came in for workshops.





Research:



Desktop & Interviews.



Interviewed 3 current users and 7 potential users.



Breaking down the research into findings.





Findings for millennials related to content consumption:



Lack of interest in the magazine format.

Prefers tailored content.

Dislikes annoying ads.

Prefers less friction.

Millennials actually likes to read, in an article format.

Prefers images that can explain content.

Goldfish attention span.
